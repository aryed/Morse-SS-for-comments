Our main goal is the following: Let $\fC$ be a framed flow category. Let $n,p,r$ and let $S_n$ be the flow category with one object at degree $n$. Let $W:S_n \to \fC$ be a left flow module over $\fC_{p,p-r}$. Let $V:\fC_{[p,p-r]} \to K_{p-(r+1)}}$ be a right flow module over $\fC$ which induces from the flow category $\fC_{[p,p-(r+1)]}$. Then (by abuse of notation) we have maps $W,V$ of coherent chain complexes $K_{S_n} \to K_{\fC_{[p,p-r]}}$ and $K_{\fC_{[p,p-r]}} \to K_{p-(r+1)}$. We want to show that the composition of these two maps $V \circ W:K_{S_n} \to K_{p-(r+1)}$ is given by 
$$\sum_{s=p-r}^{p} V_s \circ (id_{I^{\wedge (p-(p-r))}}) \wedge W_s : I^r \wedge \mathbb{S} \to K_{p-(r+1)}$$
where we treat
$$V_s:I^{\wedge (s-(p-r))} \wedge K_s\to K_{p-(r+1)}$$
and
$$W_s:I^{\wedge (p-s)} \wedge \mathbb{S}\to K_s$$
This is the Pontaryagin-Thom collapse map of the manifold:
$$\bigsqcup V_s \times W_s$$.
